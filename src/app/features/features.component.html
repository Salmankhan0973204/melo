MovingOn Features

Drive to Earn

Users who own transport vehicles such as motorbikes and cars can participate in Drive activities to receive rewards. This activity is suitable for technology taxi drivers (Uber, Lyft, Grab, Didi, Ola,...).

Move to Earn

Users can exercise and get rewards through activities: Walking, Running and Cycling.

NFT

These are tokens that have been designed to be unique. They are used to identify a specific owner. Once you own the NFT, you can then use it or sell it as you please on the Marketplace.

Staking

MovingOn Finance will implement a Staking product so that MOVON token holders can earn passive profits. Staking is essential to maintaining a safe and healthy network.
